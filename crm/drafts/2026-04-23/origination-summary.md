## Category Breakdown

- **Roofers** (22): Strong coverage across all counties. Focus on coastal/storm-exposed areas where insurance claims are common.
- **Plumbers** (10): Good coverage in major metros (PBC, Broward, Miami-Dade). Lower urgency for public adjuster outreach than roofers.
- **HVAC** (9): Established in PBC, Broward, Miami-Dade. AC failures often trigger water/mold damage claims.
- **Water Mitigation** (6): Solid in PBC & Broward. These are direct B2B referral partners; high-value for adjuster relationships.
- **Mold Remediation** (5): Lesser coverage than water mitigation. Often follows water damage jobs—strong secondary referral play.
- **General Contractors** (2): Intentionally sparse (many are regional chains). GCs with small-job focus harder to target.
- **P&C Attorneys** (3): All public adjusters in PBC area. **Gap:** Should add insurance defense attorneys & claims counsel in other counties.

- **Property Managers** (7): Mostly PBC & Broward residential. **Gap:** Underrepresented in Miami-Dade & coastal Brevard-adjacent areas.
