## High-priority: **Roof inspection / engineering inspector firms** (NEW CATEGORY)

HB 815 takes effect **July 1, 2026** — 30 days out. The bill creates an "authorized inspector" pathway for roofs 15+ years old: if an inspector certifies a steep-slope roof has ≥5 years of useful life, the carrier cannot deny coverage on roof-age grounds. This **manufactures demand** for licensed roof inspectors in PBC starting July 1.

Suggested category slug: `roof-inspector` (sibling of `roofer`)

Search targets (PBC / Martin / Broward / Miami-Dade):
- Licensed FL engineering firms doing residential roof inspections
- PEs offering "wind mitigation" + "useful life" certifications
- Existing 4-point inspection companies (likely to add HB 815 inspections as a product line)

**Why it matters for the referral ecosystem:** PA → inspector → roofer pipeline. Homeowner with a coverage threat goes to PA, PA refers inspector to keep the roof, inspector either certifies (we send to roofer for repairs later) or fails (we send straight to roofer for replacement). Triangular referral flow.
