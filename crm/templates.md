# Outreach Templates

All templates include CAN-SPAM opt-out language. Personalization uses placeholders from organizations.csv.

## Tone Escalation by Stage

- **initial:** Friendly intro, establish credibility, propose light collaboration
- **followup-1:** Value-add (data, insight, case study), reinforce partnership angle
- **followup-2:** Direct ask (meeting, call), urgency signal, social proof
- **escalation:** Channel switch (LinkedIn if email silent, phone if LinkedIn silent)
- **meeting-scheduled:** Logistics, calendar link, call prep

## Compliance Notes

- Zero language that could read as PA solicitation under FL Stat. 626.854 (applies to service providers, not victims, but stay clear)
- Every template includes CAN-SPAM opt-out
- No deceptive subject lines
- LinkedIn: short, professional, no mass-blast feel
