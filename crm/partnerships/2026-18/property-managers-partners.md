## Strategic Context

Property managers and HOA managers are **multipliers** — one relationship generates referrals across an entire portfolio. Key signals this week:

1. **Condo buyer's market in Broward** (11.3 months supply) — property managers handling condo associations are under pressure to keep buildings in good shape, which means claims are top of mind.
2. **Depopulated policies affect managed buildings.** A condo association or rental portfolio that shifted off Citizens in 2023–2024 has a new carrier that handles claims differently. The property manager is the one who discovers this the hard way.
3. **FHA approval bottleneck:** Only 0.9% of South Florida condo buildings are FHA-approved. Buildings with unresolved storm damage or deferred maintenance can lose FHA approval — a PA who helps maximize claim recovery keeps buildings fundable.
4. **Luxury market growth:** Property managers handling $2M+ properties have the most to gain from a PA who understands high-value claim documentation.
5. **Palm Beach civil litigation surge:** Palm Beach insurance dispute filings are elevated. Property managers caught in disputes need a PA partner before they need an attorney.
