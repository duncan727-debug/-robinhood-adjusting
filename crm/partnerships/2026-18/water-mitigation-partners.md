## Strategic Context

This is the **highest-urgency category** this week. Three converging signals:

1. **April rainfall 3x normal** since April 1 in Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach. Remediation firms are booked through summer.
2. **Coverage-straddle disputes active now:** "Was it sudden water or slow seepage?" is the claim question carriers use to deny. Remediators are doing jobs where the insurance payout doesn't cover full scope.
3. **Drought-to-wet sequencing** elevates plumbing/slow-leak claims — the exact damage type most frequently misclassified as "gradual" (and denied).

Remediation firms don't need more leads right now — they're full. What they need is a PA in the loop **before scoping** so the insurance math works and they actually get paid for what they scope.

**The pitch is not "I'll send you work." The pitch is "I'll make your current work actually pay out."**
