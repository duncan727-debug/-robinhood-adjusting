## Strategic Context

- **SB 808 goes live July 1:** Any home inspector or contractor can now do a carrier-accepted roof inspection. Roofers who lose scope to bad inspections need a PA who can dispute.
- **Pre-season window closes June 1:** 33 days to build partnerships before the market goes reactive.
- **Roofing labor shortage:** Contractors are turning away work — they want clean referrals and clean claims, not jobs where the insurance doesn't cover scope.
- **Insurance rate relief (11–14% drops):** Lower premiums don't mean lower claim values. Roofers still need a PA on disputed scope.
- **All Phase Construction** is actively expanding in Deerfield Beach — growth-mode contractors are the best partnership targets.
