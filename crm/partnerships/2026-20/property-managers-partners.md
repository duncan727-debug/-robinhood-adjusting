## Strategic Insight: Multi-Property Portfolio Advantage
Property managers offer a unique advantage: **coordinated claim handling across multiple properties owned by same entity** (e.g., investor with 5+ homes, or HOA with 45+ units).

Duncan should develop a "portfolio claims coordinator" service:
- When one property owner has claims, coordinate across all their properties
- Maximize cumulative recovery (economies of scale for insurer negotiation)
- Offer discount pricing for multi-property claims (e.g., 18% instead of 20% PA fee)
- This differentiates Duncan from single-property PAs
