## Strategic Context: SB 808 Roof-to-Wall Scope Expansion

- Duncan's claims include roof-to-wall assessment (new requirement under SB 808)
- General contractors can now bid roof-to-wall connection upgrades as part of repair work
- Early positioning with GCs = better claims estimates and faster project completion
