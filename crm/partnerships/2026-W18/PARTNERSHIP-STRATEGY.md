## Risk Mitigation

**What could go wrong:**

1. **Low referral rates:** Partners sign but don't refer
   - *Mitigation:* Weekly check-ins first month, co-marketing support, proof-of-value case studies

2. **Commission disputes:** Partners expect higher payouts
   - *Mitigation:* Clear written agreements, transparent tracking, quarterly reviews

3. **Service delivery issues:** Duncan can't handle referral volume
   - *Mitigation:* Start with conservative targets, scale partnership commitments as capacity grows

4. **Partner turnover:** Key decision-makers leave firms
   - *Mitigation:* Develop relationships with #2 contact person, formalize agreements with firm (not individual)

5. **Market shifts:** Claims volume drops seasonally
   - *Mitigation:* Diversify across categories, target multiple firm sizes, pilot different commission models
