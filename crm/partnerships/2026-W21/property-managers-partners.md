## Market Context (this week's signals)

- **Fannie/Freddie 15% reserve floor (effective Jan 4, 2027):** Associations below 15% face mortgage lending obstacles for unit sales. ~7 months to reach compliance. **The new PA-adjacent conversation:** "If your association can't hit 15% reserves, your unit becomes harder to sell to buyers using conforming financing." This is a coverage-AND-resale conversation, not just a claim conversation.
- **Florida PERS retirement (already in effect):** New attached condo projects no longer need Project Eligibility Review Service — Full Review delegated to lenders. Immediate relief for new project lending in PBC/Broward/Miami-Dade.
- **HB 1021 mandatory document disclosure** (effective Jan 1, 2026) + **2024 reserve-funding mandate** (post-Surfside) + **special assessments peaking 2026–2028** = stacking pressure on association budgets.
- **Wildfire exposure:** Active smoke in western Broward + south Miami-Dade. PMs/HOAs in evacuation-risk zones are fielding resident complaints NOW. Pre-loss documentation = the value-add.
- **Master policy gaps:** Multi-unit master policies frequently underinsure for wildfire + flood. Free 15-minute review = the door-opener.
