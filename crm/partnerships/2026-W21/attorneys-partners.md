- **Conflict screen:** Never refer to defense-side firms (firms representing carriers). Verify policyholder-side practice before any referral.
- **Slower close cycle than other categories:** Attorney partnerships take 6-12 weeks to mature. Plant seeds now; harvest in July-September.
- **No newsletter mentions of attorneys by name** without explicit consent — bar advertising risk.
