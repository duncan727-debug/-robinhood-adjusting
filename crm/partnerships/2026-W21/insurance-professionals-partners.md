## What makes insurance-pro partnerships different from contractor partnerships

- **Slower close cycle:** Agents are skeptical of PA referrals (regulatory + relationship sensitivity). Allow 4-8 weeks per partnership vs 2-3 weeks for contractor partnerships.
- **Higher reciprocal value:** Once an agent trusts you, they refer 5-15 claim-dispute clients/year. That's $5k-$30k in PA fees from a single agent relationship.
- **Critical no-go zone:** Never appear to be in competition with the agent's commission or to be questioning their advice. Always frame: "Your renewal commission stays with you. I represent the client at claim time."
