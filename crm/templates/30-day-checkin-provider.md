# Template: 30-day partner check-in

**Use when:** 30 days have passed since a partner's `directory_listing_status` flipped to `listed`. Automated send by `partner_lifecycle.py` (daily cron).

**Format rule:** Short, no pressure. Genuinely curious. Easy to ignore if they're busy, easy to respond to if they have something to say.

**Parameters to fill in:**
- `{FIRSTNAME}` — partner first name
- `{COMPANY}` — business name
- `{REFERRAL_COUNT}` — number of referrals Duncan has sent them so far (0 if none)

---

**Subject:** Quick check-in — month one on the directory

Hi {FIRSTNAME},

Hard to believe it's already been a month since {COMPANY} joined the directory.

Two quick questions, reply with whatever applies:

**1) From my end:** I've sent you {REFERRAL_COUNT} referral(s) so far. If anything came through that wasn't a fit — wrong scope, wrong area, bad timing — let me know so I can recalibrate.

**2) From your end:** Anything change? New service area, new phone, new team member handling intake, new specialty? Want to make sure the listing still reflects how you actually work.

That's it — no agenda, just keeping the network healthy.

If everything's good, no need to reply. I'll check in again next quarter.
