How to approach these clubs

Private clubs almost never post entry-level seasonal roles publicly — they hire by walk-in, referral, and a polite phone call to the General Manager's office. The play for a 15-year-old is:
  1. Print the résumé on good paper, dress neatly (collared shirt, no athletic wear), bring a pen.
  2. Walk into the clubhouse on a weekday morning (Tue–Thu, 9:30–11:00 a.m.) and ask the front desk who handles seasonal hiring — usually the Clubhouse Manager, F&B Manager, or Aquatics Director.
  3. Hand the résumé to a human, get their name, send a short thank-you email the same day.
  4. If a club's careers page lists openings — apply online and drop in.
