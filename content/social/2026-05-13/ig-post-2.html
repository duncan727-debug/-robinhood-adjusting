Know The Difference

Insurance adjuster vs. Public adjuster

×
Insurance adjuster
Works for the carrier
  • ×Paid by the insurance company
  • ×Goal: settle for the carrier's number
  • ×Reads the policy in the carrier's favor
  • ×You meet them after damage occurs
✓
Public adjuster
Works for YOU
  • ✓Paid only from your settlement
  • ✓Goal: what your policy actually covers
  • ✓Licensed by Florida DFS
  • ✓No upfront cost. Free review.
