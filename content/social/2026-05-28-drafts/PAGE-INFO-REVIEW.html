About (long)
Robinhood Adjusting is a Florida-licensed public adjusting practice serving South Florida property owners — Palm Beach, Martin, Broward, and Miami-Dade counties. We represent you, the policyholder, in property insurance claims: documenting damage, reading your policy, writing the estimate to the carrier's standards, and negotiating settlement. We are not the insurance company's adjuster. We are not a contractor. We are not an attorney. When a claim doesn't need a public adjuster, we tell you straight — before you sign anything. Founded and operated by Duncan Littlejohn, Wellington, FL.
