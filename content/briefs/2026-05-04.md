## Real Estate & Wealth Migration

### Luxury Inventory Tightens; Relocating HNW Buyers Face 60–90 Day Closings

Palm Beach (+29% YoY sales volume to $2.6B year-to-date) and Miami-Dade luxury segments (+19.6% SFR, avg $3.17M) continue to absorb wealth migration faster than local supply can scale. The market dynamic: relocating high-net-worth buyers are unfamiliar with Florida insurance, mitigation requirements, and hurricane-season claims exposure. Average transaction timeline: 60–90 days from offer to close.

**Timing opportunity:** A relocating HNW buyer closing in July or August is signing the insurance policy and documenting property condition in May and June. They are acutely risk-aware (moving $2M+ asset to a hurricane zone) and will engage advisors during the due-diligence phase.

**PA positioning:** "Welcome to South Florida. Your new property is valuable and exposed. Before you close, let's audit your insurance coverage and document pre-loss conditions so you're not surprised when claims file."

**Contractor positioning:** Mitigation inspections and premium upgrades (impact windows, roof reinforcement, backup power) during the 60–90 day transaction window lock in relationships and recurring service revenue.
