## Caption

Two contractors in Palm Beach Gardens just found out they were NEVER bonded. 😳

They paid $90K+ for surety bonds. The agent allegedly kept the money. The carrier never saw a premium. The contractors had no idea — until the arrest hit the news.

The brutal lesson: "I paid for it" ≠ "I'm covered."

Your 15-minute protection plan:
✅ Call the surety carrier DIRECTLY — confirm every bond is in force
✅ Check your agent's carrier appointments (not just the license) at the DFS license search
✅ On new bonds, get confirmation from the carrier, not the agent

If a bonded job fails while you're unknowingly unbonded, that exposure is YOURS.

Forward this to a contractor you know. It's a 15-minute check that could save their business.
