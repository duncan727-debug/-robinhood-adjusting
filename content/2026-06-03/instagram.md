**211 days until the Florida Building Code 9th Edition kicks in.** ⏱️

If you're a contractor in South Florida, the permit-pull date is the whole game now. Pull before Dec 31, 2026 → 8th Edition through completion. Pull after → 9th Edition, new wind-load tables, tighter sealed-deck rules.

For Miami-Dade and Broward HVHZ work, it's even sharper — the 30% rule can drag the whole envelope into current code on a roof replacement. That's a $25K job turning into a $60K conversation.

Two moves worth making this week:
→ Front-load discretionary projects into the pull-forward window
→ Get a written code-effective-date plan from your material distributors

The contractors who plan this now win Q1 2027. The ones who don't are going to spend January re-quoting jobs.
