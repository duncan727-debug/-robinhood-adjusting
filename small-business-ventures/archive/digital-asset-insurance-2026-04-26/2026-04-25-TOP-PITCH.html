Execution Gaps & Mitigation

Key Risks & How to Handle Them

Gap: Underwriter Integration Complexity
Mitigation: Partner with broker tech vendor (e.g., Cowbell, Canopius API team) who has existing integrations. Use their SDKs. Start with REST APIs, move to middleware only if volume justifies.
Gap: Regulatory Uncertainty in LatAm
Mitigation: Start in Brazil (most developed fintech regulation). Hire local compliance expert ($5K/month). Partner with regulated local brokers for local operations. Marketplace = zero custody risk.
Gap: Crypto Market Volatility (Insurance Demand Swings)
Mitigation: Premium demand actually RISES in bear markets (holders protect capital). Staking insurance (yield protection) = counter-cyclical. Build product mix across bull/bear scenarios.
Gap: Talent for Development
Mitigation: Hire 1-2 senior engineers (remote, crypto-background preferred). Build MVP with no-code tools first (Zapier, Make) while hiring. Use dev shop ($30K MVP) if timeline-critical.
Gap: Customer Acquisition at Scale
Mitigation: Early customers come from underwriter referrals (white-label). Crypto communities (Reddit, Discord) provide organic growth. Affiliate model with custody providers (Kraken, Celsius rebuilds). By month 9, partnerships drive 60% of volume.
