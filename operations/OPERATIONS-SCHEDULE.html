Key Notes

  • Two-Day Prospecting Cycle: Contacts scraped Day 1 (9am) → enriched (10am) → intel profiles Day 2 (5am) → personalized drafts Day 2 (6am) → outreach Day 2 (8am–3pm).
  • 4-Batch Outreach: Drafts are spread across 8am, 10:30am, 12:30pm, and 3pm to avoid spam signals and spread engagement windows.
  • Schedule: Operations cadence runs Mon–Sat. Special days: Partnership Builder (Wed), Weekly Rollup (Fri), Weekly Trends generation (Sat). Sundays now have one job: the Weekly Trends email send at 9am — Saturday's brief is site-only (no email) and Sunday delivers the synthesis to all subscribers.
  • Auto-Recovery: LaunchAgent catches any missed jobs when the Mac wakes. State tracking via lock files and log entries prevents duplicate runs.
  • HubSpot Fully Automated: CRM uploads via API — no manual import steps required. Daily master CSV generated + uploaded at 8:30am.
  • Drip vs. Deal Pipeline: Response Handler routes warm replies to the HubSpot Deal Pipeline; non-responders and soft declines enter the 3-touch Drip Queue (runs daily at 9am).
