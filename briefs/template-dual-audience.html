What This Means For Your Business

New Construction = New Work Opportunities
That $2.8B development pipeline means contractors, electricians, HVAC, and specialty trades are in demand. Property management companies and business service providers are ramping up to service these new projects.
Growing Businesses Need Your Services
With employment up and new companies relocating to South Florida, demand is growing for accounting, legal, insurance, staffing, and consulting services. This is lead generation season for B2B service providers.
Commercial Finance Opening Doors
Regional banks are actively lending. Business owners are financing expansions and new ventures. If you work with growth-stage companies, now is the time to connect with them through referral partners and networking.
Referral Partner Opportunity
With rapid growth, service professionals are busier than ever. Look for partnership opportunities with accountants, brokers, contractors, and consultants who are referring business to complementary service providers.
