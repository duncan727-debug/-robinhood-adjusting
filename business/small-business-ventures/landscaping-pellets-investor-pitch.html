Operations & Logistics

Supply Chain

  • Collection: Partner with landscapers for regular waste pickup
  • Drying: Sun-dry waste 1-2 weeks before processing
  • Processing: Chip → Dry → Pelletize (2-4 tons raw = 1 ton pellets)
  • Storage: Weather-protected storage for finished product

Location Requirements

  • Land: 2-3 acres (waste staging, drying, storage)
  • Power: Single-phase electric for pelletizer
  • Access: Truck access for material delivery
  • Zoning: Industrial or agricultural (South Florida has abundant options)
