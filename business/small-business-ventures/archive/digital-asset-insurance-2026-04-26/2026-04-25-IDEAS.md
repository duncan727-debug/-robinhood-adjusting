# New Business Ideas | April 25, 2026

## 1. Crypto-Backed Real Estate Financing Platform
**Pitch:** White-label lending platform that enables construction companies and developers in LatAm to access capital using crypto holdings as collateral, reducing reliance on traditional banking while providing USD/USDC liquidity. Recurring revenue via 1-2% origination fees + servicing spreads.

**Market Size:** 
- Global construction financing market: $2.5T+
- LatAm real estate construction growth: 15-20% CAGR through 2028
- Addressable (crypto-collateralized): $50-100B

**Unit Economics:**
- Average loan size: $500K-$2M
- Origination fee: 1.5% ($7.5K-$30K per deal)
- Annual servicing spread: 1-2% of AUM
- Customer cost: ~$5K acquisition + compliance setup
- Margin: 60-70% on origination + 75% on servicing
- Customer payback: 3-4 months on origination + recurring

**Feasibility Score:** 8/10
- Needs regulatory partnerships (LatAm banking); Duncan has insurance contacts who know regulators
- Requires fintech infrastructure ($200K-$500K MVP); Duncan has crypto tech networks
- Compliance complexity HIGH but insurance/PA background helps position

**Why It Works:**
- **Crypto expertise** → understands collateral valuation, custody, liquidation risks
- **Real estate + insurance connections** → can approach developers, insurers (AIG, Chubb) as partners
- **LatAm positioning** → Portuguese/Spanish + network = direct to developers in Brazil, Mexico
- **Recurring revenue** → servicing fees compound monthly across growing AUM

**Market Size:**
- Global institutional crypto AUM: $200B+
- LatAm high-net-worth crypto adoption: 12-18% growth annually
- Addressable (LatAm ultra-HNW + institutions): $3-8B in assets seeking custody

**Unit Economics:**
- AUM under management per customer: $10M-$50M average
- Management fee: 0.25-0.5% AUM ($25K-$250K annually per customer)
- Customer acquisition cost: $15K-$30K (partnership-driven)
- Margin: 65-75% (ops scales well post-infrastructure)
- Payback period: 12-18 months

**Feasibility Score:** 7/10
- Regulatory capital requirements ($5M+ regulatory reserve)

- Insurance liability partnerships critical but doable with PA connections

**Why It Works:**
- **Crypto expertise** → secure protocol design + investor confidence
- **Real estate/insurance network** → position via RIA/family office advisors, insurance carriers
- **LatAm edge** → exclusive access to wealth manager networks (Portuguese-speaking)
- **Recurring** → AUM-based fees scale passively

## 3. Real Estate Underwriting Intelligence Platform (SaaS)
**Pitch:** API-driven platform that aggregates climate risk, structural data, title history, and insurance loss data for underwriters evaluating commercial real estate. Reduces claim costs by 15-20% through better risk pricing.

**Market Size:**
- Commercial real estate market: $16T global, $1.5T LatAm
- Active underwriters: 300K+ globally, 25K+ in LatAm
- Addressable (underwriters + brokers): $2-5B in annual spend

**Unit Economics:**
- Target customer: regional insurers, brokers (50-500 agents)
- SaaS pricing: $500-$2K per agent/month ($6K-$24K annually)
- Customer acquisition: $8K-$15K (direct sales + partnerships)
- Margin: 70-80% (software scales)
- Payback: 6-9 months

**Feasibility Score:** 8/10
- Data sources exist (public records, satellite imagery, NOAA); integrations needed
- Sales to underwriters comfortable (PA background = instant credibility)
- Compliance straightforward (no custody, no lending, just analytics)

**Why It Works:**
- **Insurance connections** → existing underwriter relationships from PA work
- **Real estate expertise** → understands underwriting workflows + pain points
- **Crypto + fintech skills** → API design, data pipeline, blockchain for immutable records
- **LatAm advantage** → can position as "data platform built for LatAm risk profiles"

- Commission: 15-20% ($750-$10K per customer annually)
