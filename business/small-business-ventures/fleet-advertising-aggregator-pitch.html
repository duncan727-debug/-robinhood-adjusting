Opportunity: South Florida's logistics boom creates $500M+ in annual truck advertising demand. By aggregating local fleet operators and connecting them with premium advertisers, we capture 20% commission ($80-100/truck/month) with no inventory or production costs—pure software margin.

The Problem & Market Opportunity

Fleet Operators' Problem

  • Unused Asset: Trucks sit idle 40% of day; sides are blank billboards
  • No Direct Access: Don't know advertisers directly; hard to monetize
  • Fragmented Supply: Small fleets (20-100 trucks) can't negotiate directly with brands
  • Logistics Friction: No one to manage campaigns, payments, installation

Advertiser Demand

  • CPM: $0.76 (cheapest ad medium—beats digital, TV, radio)
  • Market Size: $3.8B vehicle wrap market, 9.5% CAGR
  • Fleet Ads High-ROI: Targeting high-traffic routes (I-95, Port of Miami)
  • Easy Metrics: GPS tracking + impression measurement = data-driven buying

Competitive Advantage

vs. Direct Sales (TRUCK ADS, Crosswalk)

  • Faster Growth: Our aggregation model reaches fleets 10x faster than door-to-door sales
  • Better Margins: They sell to fleets at fixed rates; we scale on commission
  • Easier for Fleets: We handle everything—they just provide trucks

vs. Pure Marketplace (Wrapify)

  • B2B Focus: Wrapify targets personal vehicles; we own commercial truck market
  • Relationship Sales: Fleet owners want a dedicated account rep, not a self-serve app
  • Higher LTV: One fleet = 50+ trucks; Wrapify gets $150-400/month per driver

  • Risk: Advertisers won't pay premium rates for truck ads
  • Mitigation: Market proven ($3.8B global, $0.76 CPM); start with brand partners we know will commit

Supply Risk

  • Risk: Fleets unwilling to participate
  • Mitigation: Free to fleets, 80% of revenue; instant pilot programs prove value before scaling

Execution Risk
