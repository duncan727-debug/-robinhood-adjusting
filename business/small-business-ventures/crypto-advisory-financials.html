Business Model Assumptions

Revenue Streams

  • Strategic Retainers: $5-10K/month per client
  • Avg Retainer Client Value: $120K/year
  • Due Diligence Engagements: $10-30K per engagement
  • Placement Fees: 2-5% of capital raised
  • Other Consulting: $50-150K per regulatory/compliance project
  • Gross Margin: 80-95% (services business)

Client Acquisition

  • CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): $500-2,000 per client (relationship-based, low paid advertising)
  • Sales Cycle: 2-4 weeks (warm network, trusted advisor positioning)
  • Client Retention: 90%+ (mission-critical service)
  • Upsell Potential: High (placement fees, additional projects)
  • Scaling Model: Linear (each retainer client adds $10K/month recurring)
Key Assumption: Revenue model is heavily retainer-based initially (80% Year 1), with placement fees and additional consulting upside that accelerates in Years 2-3. Gross margins are exceptional (80%+) because it's a services business with minimal COGS.

Summary & Investment Thesis

Why This Business Works

  • Exceptional Unit Economics: LTV:CAC of 330:1. Every client acquired pays back in 3 days and generates $110K annual profit.
  • Proven Market: $200B+ LATAM crypto market, 500+ protocols, all wanting US institutional access. Demand is real and urgent.
  • Unique Positioning: Only trilingual (English/Portuguese/Spanish) crypto advisor with MBA, institutional investor experience, and LATAM network.
  • Fast to Revenue: Month 2 (first client), break-even by Month 3, $35K+ monthly profit by Month 6.
  • Low Capital Required: $20K investment with 6-7 months runway. Near zero fixed costs besides your time.
  • Scalable Model: Each additional client adds $10K/month recurring revenue with minimal incremental cost.
  • Multiple Revenue Streams: Retainers (predictable), project work (upside), placement fees (big upside).
Risk Assessment:
  • Market Risk (Very Low): LATAM crypto demand proven; US institutional capital proven. No demand uncertainty.
  • Execution Risk (Low): Warm network (500+ connections), proven track record at Gate.io/0xScope, trusted in ecosystem.
  • Competition Risk (Low-Medium): Moat = trilingual + crypto expertise + LATAM network + MBA. Hard to replicate in <2 years.
  • Crypto Risk (Low): Advisory services are counter-cyclical; most valuable during downturns when institutional capital looks for value.
3-Year Outcome:

With base case assumptions (8 clients Y1, 15 Y2, 20 Y3), cumulative 3-year profit is $2.94M. This represents 147x return on $20K investment (23,925% ROI). Even conservative scenario (4-6-8 clients) yields $700K+ 3-year profit (35x return).
