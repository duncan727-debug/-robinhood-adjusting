Summary & Recommendations

Investment Thesis

  • Low Capital Barrier: $9,500 equipment + $5,500 working capital = $15,000 total. Payback in 8-12 months at base case.
  • Free Feedstock Advantage: Raw material costs are $0 (landscaper waste). 95.7% gross margin vs. 30-50% for traditional pellet businesses.
  • Year-Round Operation: South Florida climate enables 365-day waste supply (unlike northern states with seasonal waste).
  • Scalable Model: Additional $2,500 machines can expand capacity to 600+ tons/year. Each new machine compounds profitability.
  • Multiple Exit Paths: Can scale to 1000+ tons/year, sell to energy companies, or license model to other regions.
Key Risks & Mitigations:
  • Feedstock Supply: Establish contracts with 5+ landscaping contractors before launching.
  • Price Volatility: Lock in long-term buyer contracts at $300/ton or higher; diversify customer base.
  • Equipment Failure: Budget $1,000/year for maintenance; have backup supplier relationship.
  • Market Acceptance: Start with bagged product for landscapers (easy market entry); then scale to bulk buyers.
