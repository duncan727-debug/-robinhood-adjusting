Strategy

Posting Strategy by Group Type

Community Groups (Tier 1)

  • Week 1: Join + comment on 3–5 threads. Don't post yet.
  • Week 2: Share hurricane checklist as resource (not an ad)
  • Week 3: Answer a question; reference the free guide
  • Ongoing: Max once per week

Real Estate & Investor (Tier 2)

  • Observe for 3–5 days first
  • First post: market data from the daily brief
  • Build credibility before pitching PA services
  • Target: distressed property discussions, claims history questions

Contractor Groups (Tier 3)

  • Direct approach: "We work with contractors on claim documentation."
  • Share the contractor vetting guide as a homeowner resource
  • Message: "If you pass all 12 checks, you'll love this document."

HOA / Property Managers (Tier 5)

  • Position as industry resource, not a vendor
  • Offer to speak at HOA meetings or write a Q&A column
  • Leave-behind: insurance policy review checklist
