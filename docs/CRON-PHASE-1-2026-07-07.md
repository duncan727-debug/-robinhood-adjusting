- Weekly trends job creates a local draft only and should not publish in Phase 1.

## Reply Sweep Filter Fix

The morning and afternoon reply/bounce sweeps were paused on 2026-07-07 after the first afternoon run misclassified newsletter/vendor mail as replies and queued a beehiiv message for answer parsing. `scripts/imap_bridge.py` was patched the same day to skip beehiiv entirely, skip bulk/list/newsletter/vendor headers, skip known vendor domains, and require reply evidence before HubSpot is touched. The jobs were then re-enabled for their next scheduled runs.
