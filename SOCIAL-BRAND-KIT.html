About / Long Description

Robinhood Adjusting is a licensed public adjusting firm serving Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, Martin, St. Lucie, Indian River, and Brevard counties.

We represent property owners — not insurance companies — through the claims process. Built on a simple premise: the money your insurance policy promises you should actually reach you.

Most public adjusters come from contracting. We approach claims as financial analysts. That means a documented file, a defensible valuation, and a settlement that reflects what your policy actually covers — not the carrier's first offer.

No upfront cost. No obligation until you decide to move forward.

07 Cross-Posting Setup (Meta Business Suite)

Once both accounts exist:

  1. Log into business.facebook.com → Business Settings
  2. Create a Business Portfolio: Robinhood Adjusting
  3. Add the FB Page → add the IG account → link them
  4. In Instagram on phone: Settings → Account → Branded content → connect FB page
  5. Enable "Crosspost to Instagram" in FB Business Suite Composer
