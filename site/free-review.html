What's Included & What's Not

Honest Expectations, Up Front

We built this as a service to property owners who don't know whether they have a claim worth filing. Here's exactly what you'll get — and what you won't.

✓ What you get on the call

  • 15-minute Zoom call with a licensed Florida public adjuster
  • Walk-through of your damage or letter on camera
  • Honest read on whether the loss is likely a covered insurable event
  • Plain-language explanation of what your policy probably covers (and doesn't)
  • Practical next steps — including when you can handle it yourself

✕ What we won't do

  • Ask you to sign a representation contract on the call
  • Pressure you into hiring us — most calls end with "you can probably handle this yourself"
  • Give you a binding coverage determination (only your carrier can)
  • Replace your insurer's adjuster or your attorney
  • Charge you anything — this is free regardless of outcome
