What's In the Checklist

A preview of the 39 items — organized into 5 sections covering every angle of hurricane preparedness.

📋 Insurance Review

  • Know your hurricane deductible (it's not what you think)
  • Locate and digitize your policy documents
  • Check coverage limits vs. current replacement cost
  • Understand flood coverage gaps
  • Save a public adjuster's number now, not after the storm

🏠 Exterior & Structural

  • Roof inspection — the #1 vulnerability
  • Storm shutter / impact glass operational check
  • Garage door wind rating — the most common failure point
  • Tree trimming within 10 feet
  • HVAC strapping and anchor bolts

🎒 Emergency Preparedness

  • 7-day water and food supply
  • Generator safety (CO poisoning kills every season)
  • 30-day medication supply
  • Know your evacuation zone (A–F)
  • Cash — ATMs fail after storms

📞 After the Storm — Protect Your Claim

  • Document damage BEFORE any cleanup
  • Do NOT sign an AOB with any contractor
  • Make only emergency temporary repairs
  • Call a PA before your insurance company
  • How to request your full claim file
