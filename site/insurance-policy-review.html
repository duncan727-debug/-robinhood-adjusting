What's in the Checklist

A snapshot of the 12 items — covering the coverage gaps that cost South Florida homeowners the most.

💰 Coverage Limits

  • Is your dwelling coverage keeping up with rebuild costs?
  • Replacement cost vs. actual cash value — the difference is thousands
  • Extended replacement cost endorsement — do you have it?

🌀 Hurricane Deductibles

  • Your hurricane deductible may be 2–5% of your home's value
  • Named storm vs. hurricane trigger — which activates your higher deductible?
  • Wind mitigation discounts — are they applied to your policy?

🌊 Flood & Water

  • Standard policies do NOT cover flood damage
  • Water backup / sewer endorsement — often excluded by default

🏗 Code & Liability

  • Ordinance & Law coverage — critical for pre-2002 homes
  • Personal property sub-limits on jewelry and electronics
  • Liability limits — do you have a pool, dog, or significant assets?
