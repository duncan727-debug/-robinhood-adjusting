Weekly trend reports published to the site are public-facing market intelligence. Never include:
- Priority Prospects / CRM outreach lists
- Content run suggestions or hooks
- Operational recommendations or urgent task lists
- Any mention of Duncan by name or as the audience
- Footer language like "Feeds 9am content run and 12pm CRM outreach run"

**Why:** These pages are live at robinhoodadjusting.com/trends/. Including internal strategy or personal action items looks unprofessional to visitors and exposes business methods.

**How to apply:** Each weekly trend HTML should end after "What Changed This Week" (or the last market-data section). Strip everything after that before publishing to site/trends/. The internal/ops version can stay in content/weekly/ if needed.
